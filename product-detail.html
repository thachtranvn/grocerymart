How to Brew Your Perfect Cup

Follow these steps for the best results:

  1. Grind the Lavazza Espresso Whole Bean Coffee to a suitable consistency for your coffee making machine.
  2. For an espresso machine, ensure the machine is clean and add the ground coffee to the portafilter.
  3. For a Moka Pot, fill the filter basket with the ground coffee and screw on the top chamber containing water.
  4. For a Drip Coffee Maker, place a coffee filter in the basket and add the ground coffee.
  5. For a French Press, add the ground coffee to the French Press and heat water to the appropriate temperature.
  6. Follow the specific instructions for your chosen coffee making machine to brew the coffee.
  7. Enjoy your Lavazza Espresso!
